Domain, Hosting, Maintenance & Service Costs

You pay for the domain & hosting costs for your website. These are the minimum requirements to have a website & incur monthly or yearly subscription fees. Also, any additional services provided to the website will have recurring fees associated with them.

For medium & larger projects, I recommend 1 year of maintenance to support any technical issues the site may have. Maintenance incurs a monthly cost of 5% of the project price a year.

Development Price

Prices are based on required work for agreed-upon design, features & options. If my prices feel slightly out of your range, I may be able to develop a site that fits your budget so please don't be afraid to reach out!

Development Agreement & Payment

Once we have a confirmed plan & price I request half of the money upfront then the other half when the website launches. If at any point after initial payment you decide you would like additional features, it will be a separate invoice that requires full payment to implement. Any features or options you decide to remove once work is started will be invoiced immediately for already applied hours & for hours required to remove said work.
